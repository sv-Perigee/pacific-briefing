From 135°E longitude East to the South American Continent

Look for the 'good news' text near the bottom:

  • A. TROPICAL CYCLONE SUMMARY: NONE.
  • B. TROPICAL DISTURBANCE SUMMARY: NONE.
  • C. SUBTROPICAL SYSTEM SUMMARY: NONE.

If there is any other detail, then you should investigate further.

Help

This resource was created to assist international itinerant sailors cruising the Western South Pacific by providing quick and easy access to key online weather and other safety-related information.
The "All on 1 Page" briefing can be updated by refreshing the page - unless otherwise identified, the linked graphics will then re-load with the latest available images.
The earthquake/tsunami status banner will auto-update every 5-minutes, and is colour-coded:
Green = All Clear
Yellow = Watch
Orange = Advisory
Red = Warning
